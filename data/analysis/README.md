## Key Insights Summary

- **23.7% overall churn rate** (above industry average)
- **45% of churn occurs in first 6 months** (critical retention window)
- **Lagos youth segment** shows highest risk (31% churn)
- **Satisfaction below 3.5** drives exponential churn increase
- **₦127M annual revenue at risk** from customer attrition

## Recommendations Impact

Implementing the top 3 recommendations could achieve:
- 15-20% churn reduction
- ₦19M annual revenue preservation
- Improved customer satisfaction scores
- Enhanced competitive positioning
